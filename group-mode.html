5-Party Team Damage Calculator

How to Setup Each Player

1. Configure Stats: Enter Attack Power, Weapon Power, Hit Rating, Crit Chance (0-100%), Crit Power (25+%), Pen Rating, and Pen Chance (0-100%).
2. Select Weapons: Choose Primary, Secondary, and Auxiliary weapons from the dropdowns.
3. Add Abilities: Click on ability slots to select 7 active abilities (including 1 elite) and 8 passive abilities (including 1 elite).
4. Configure Augments: Select augments for your abilities from the augment dropdowns.
5. Import/Export: Use the Import/Export section to save/load builds. You can import individual player builds or use Team Import/Export for all players.
Tip: Use the individual import/export for each player to quickly load pre-configured builds, or use the Team Import/Export to manage all 5 players at once.
